# Why This Meeting Matters

## Overview
A visual overview of the AEM team's mission-and-goals meeting held February 2, 2026, introducing four strategic pillars that will define the team's direction.

## Learning Objectives
The viewer will understand:
1. The context of this meeting — reorganization, workshop preparation, collaborative input
2. The four key strategic pillars being introduced
3. The collaborative spirit — the team is invited to shape the vision

## Design Instructions

### Style Preferences
- Corporate-memphis: flat vector, bright saturated colors (purple, orange, teal, yellow)
- White or light pastel background
- Disproportionate human figures, floating geometric elements, no outlines

### Layout Preferences
- Bento-grid: modular grid with varied cell sizes
- Hero cell for meeting context, medium cells for 4 pillars
- Quote and team cells as accent elements

### Other Requirements
- Landscape 16:9 (1920x1080)
- Language: English
- Clean sans-serif typography, bold headings
- NO text rendered in the image that could be illegible — use icons and visual metaphors instead of paragraph text

- Decorative: Holographic 3D star shapes, yellow smiley stickers, pink hearts

## DESIGN RULES
- Left and right halves are distinguished by color temperature (blue vs pink) not by dark vs light
- Both sides are equally appealing — balanced comparison, not one-is-better
- Cards float at slight angles on both sides
- Center "VS" is playful, not aggressive
- NOT corporate, NOT dark — warm, inclusive, team-friendly

# Accelerate AEM Growth and Adoption

## Overview
A mission-driven infographic showing how the AEM team accelerates growth through a virtuous cycle of customer engagement, rapid experimentation, AI-driven innovation, and engineering repeatable growth engines for the ecosystem.

## Learning Objectives
The viewer will understand:
1. The core mission to accelerate AEM growth and adoption through deep customer engagement
2. The cycle: discover → validate → scale → engineer breakthroughs
3. How individual breakthroughs become repeatable growth engines for the broader AEM ecosystem

Key Takeaways for Migration Planning

  • STILL dominates at ~80% of total volume due to 60+ separate country domains, each independently managed.
  • Localization is the multiplier — core unique content per brand is modest, but replication across dozens of markets drives the total up.
  • Not all pages need equal treatment — product pages, news/press, and structural pages have very different migration complexity.
  • Consider phased migration — migrate 1 flagship market per brand first, then replicate the template across remaining locales.
  • Reports subdomain (reports.kiongroup.com) contains 1,350–1,950 HTML pages of financial reports that may or may not be in scope.
  • Each separate country domain (STILL, Linde MH) is essentially a standalone project to set up in Edge Delivery Services.
  • Baoli is the quickest win — a single domain with a compact product catalog, ideal as a pilot migration.

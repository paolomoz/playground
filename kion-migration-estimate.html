Key Takeaways

Strategic observations for migration planning

Primary Insight
STILL dominates at ~80% of total volume due to 60+ separate country domains, each independently managed. This is both the largest effort and the biggest opportunity for template reuse.
Volume Driver
Localization is the multiplier — core unique content per brand is modest, but replication across dozens of markets drives the total up.
Complexity
Not all pages need equal treatment — product pages, news/press, and structural pages have very different migration complexity.
Approach
Consider phased migration — migrate 1 flagship market per brand first, then replicate the template across remaining locales.
Scope Risk
Reports subdomain contains 1,350–1,950 HTML pages of financial reports that may or may not be in scope for migration.
Quick Win
Baoli is the ideal pilot — a single domain with a compact product catalog, perfect for validating the migration approach.

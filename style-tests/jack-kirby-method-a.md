# jack-kirby

Explosive cosmic dynamism rendered in bold black ink and flat Pop-color fields — massive foreshortened fists punch straight at the viewer, energy crackles as fields of black dots (Kirby Krackle) filling negative space, and every composition bursts outward from the page with the raw kinetic grandeur of 1960s-70s Marvel and DC Fourth World comics. Figures are barrel-chested titans with squared jaws and clenched teeth, machinery is impossibly complex with exposed circuitry and Aztec-tech ornamentation, and backgrounds boil with cosmic plasma. This is the King of Comics: nothing is small, nothing is still, everything is an event.

## Visual Elements
- **Kirby Krackle energy dots**: Dense fields of black circles (varying from pinhead to marble size) floating in negative space, forming crescent shapes, energy bursts, and cosmic phenomena — these dots are THE signature Kirby element, like dark matter made visible
- **Extreme foreshortening**: Fists, feet, and weapons thrust directly at the viewer, dramatically oversized in the foreground while bodies recede sharply into the background — every pose is a forced-perspective power shot
- **Barrel-chested titanic figures**: Characters have massive squared-off torsos, thick necks, jutting square jaws, and limbs like industrial pistons; even ordinary humans look like they could bench-press a car
- **Bold black spot inking**: Large areas of pure black fill define shadow sides of faces, undersides of arms, interiors of capes, and ground shadows — not hatched, not gradated, just solid black shapes creating stark dramatic contrast
- **Impossible Kirby machinery**: Technology rendered as dense tangles of tubes, pistons, circuit traces, concentric circles, and Aztec/Mayan geometric motifs — control panels have hundreds of tiny buttons, dials, and readouts in precise grid patterns
- **Explosive debris fields**: Rocks, metal fragments, energy shards, and rubble flying outward from impact points, each piece individually outlined and foreshortened, creating a frozen moment of destruction
- **Cosmic energy arcs**: Thick curved lines of crackling power connecting figures to machines, spanning between outstretched hands, or bridging planets — rendered as bold outlines filled with graduated dot patterns
- **Squared fingers and blocky hands**: Hands are massive with thick squared-off fingers, deep knuckle lines, and prominent tendons — they grip, punch, and gesture with theatrical force
- **Dynamic speed lines**: Parallel radiating lines behind moving figures or behind impact zones, creating explosive directional energy — NOT gentle motion lines but aggressive velocity markers
- **Aztec-tech ornamentation**: Geometric patterns on helmets, armor, and machinery that blend ancient Mesoamerican stepped-pyramid motifs with science-fiction circuitry — zigzag borders, concentric squares, interlocking angular spirals
- **Collage-texture backgrounds**: Areas of photographic or abstract collage texture (stippled, marbled, cosmic nebula patterns) filling backgrounds behind figures, suggesting dimensions beyond normal reality
- **Massive headpieces and helmets**: Characters wear enormous finned helmets, elaborate crowns, and towering headgear with antenna arrays, geometric crests, and glowing energy slots
- **Extreme perspective ground planes**: Floors and terrain shown in dramatic converging perspective with grid lines, cracks, or tile patterns that emphasize the vast scale of the environment
- **Bold black panel borders**: Thick (4-6px) black borders around every panel and container, often broken by figures or energy bursts that punch through the frame boundaries

## Typography
- **Primary Typeface**: Heavy condensed sans-serif with industrial weight — Impact, Compacta, or Machine Bold; letterforms should feel like they were stamped from steel plate
- **Title Treatment**: ALL CAPS, extra bold, tightly tracked, often tilted 5-10 degrees for dynamic energy; placed inside angular burst shapes or heavy black bars with reversed-out white text; 36-48pt equivalent
- **Body Labels**: ALL CAPS bold condensed, 12-16pt, in angular caption boxes with thick black borders; white text on black or dark blue fill, or black text on yellow fill
- **Number Callouts**: Bold blocky numerals inside star-burst or angular shield shapes, thick black outlines, filled with solar yellow or cosmic red
- **Sound Effects / Emphasis**: Oversized display type (BOOM, KRAKOOM, ZAP) rendered as physical 3D block letters with thick outlines, foreshortened for impact, often breaking panel borders
- **Caption Boxes**: Angular rectangles (not rounded) with thick black borders and colored fills (yellow, cyan), containing bold condensed all-caps narration text
- **NO thin, delicate, or script typefaces**: Every letter must have the visual mass of a concrete block. NO serif fonts, NO handwriting styles, NO elegant curves

## Anti-Patterns
- **NO gentle, soft, or pastel rendering**: This is NOT watercolor, NOT pastel chalk, NOT gentle illustration. Everything is BOLD, HARD-EDGED, and HIGH-CONTRAST. **Repeat: NO soft edges, NO gentle gradients, NO muted colors**
- **NO empty negative space**: Kirby fills EVERY background with krackle dots, energy patterns, speed lines, cosmic phenomena, or collage texture. **Blank white or empty backgrounds are WRONG. Fill the space with energy**
- **NO thin line work**: Lines are THICK and BOLD. Do NOT use delicate, thin, or uniform-weight fine lines. Outlines are heavy, blacks are dense, marks are aggressive. **NO hairlines, NO fine pen work**
- **NO static, centered, symmetrical poses**: Every figure is in EXPLOSIVE MOTION — lunging, punching, leaping, recoiling. Figures do NOT stand still with arms at sides. **NO passport-photo poses, NO calm standing figures**
- **NO manga/anime style**: Do NOT use large eyes, speed-line screentone backgrounds, chibi proportions, or kawaii aesthetics. Kirby figures are MASSIVE, BLOCKY, and POWERFUL — not slender, graceful, or cute. **NO manga eyes, NO anime hair spikes**
- **NO ligne claire uniform outlines**: This is NOT Tintin. Line weight VARIES dramatically — thick bold shadows vs. thinner detail lines. There IS black spotting. There ARE heavy shadows. **NO uniform-weight democratic linework**
- **NO realistic proportions**: Figures are HEROICALLY EXAGGERATED — barrel chests, massive fists, thick necks, jutting jaws. Do NOT draw anatomically correct, normally proportioned human figures. **Exaggerate EVERYTHING**
- **NO Art Nouveau curves or organic flowing lines**: Kirby's geometry is ANGULAR, MECHANICAL, and ARCHITECTURAL — zigzags, squares, hard corners. Do NOT use flowing vines, sinuous curves, or organic tendrils. **Angular, not organic**
- **NO minimalism**: There is NO "less is more" in Kirby. MORE is more. Every surface has detail. Every background has energy. Every figure has texture. **Do NOT simplify, do NOT reduce, do NOT leave things plain**
- **NO photorealistic rendering or 3D CGI look**: This is INK ON PAPER — bold black outlines, flat color fills with occasional dot patterns. Do NOT produce glossy, rendered, lit, or ray-traced imagery. **Hand-inked comic art, NOT digital rendering**
- **NO scattered floating decorative elements**: Debris flies FROM impact points. Energy radiates FROM sources. Krackle dots cluster in FIELDS. Nothing drifts aimlessly. **Every element has a force vector**
- **NO small, timid compositions**: If a fist is in frame, it should be ENORMOUS. If a machine is shown, it should be BUILDING-SIZED. Kirby thinks in COSMIC SCALE. **Nothing is small. Nothing is modest. EVERYTHING IS EPIC**
